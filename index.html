D-CoE: Rooted in Design Excellence

The D-CoE (Design Centre of Excellence) stands as a testament to over six decades of pioneering design education at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc). Built on a legacy of innovation and expertise, it is committed to shaping the future of design while addressing the pressing needs of today’s industry and academia.

A Legacy of Design Education

At the heart of the D-CoE lies IISc's 60-year history of excellence in design education. This legacy includes:

Graduate-Level Courses

IISc's early adoption of advanced design courses positioned it as a leader in the academic design landscape.

The MDes Program

Over 26 years, the Master of Design program has produced industry trailblazers who hold leadership roles in top companies like Maruti and JCB. This program demonstrates IISc's ability to align education with real-world needs.

The Department of Design and Manufacturing

Integral to IISc's contributions to design, recognized as one of India's top-ranked design schools, DM has set benchmarks in:

Pioneering Education

Introduced India's first PhD program in design, setting benchmarks for academic and research excellence.

Industry Innovation

Delivered groundbreaking ideas that predate even global leaders like IDEO at Stanford.

Global Recognition

Consistently ranked among the top three art and design schools in India by QS rankings.

Expertise and Practical Training

Building on this foundation, the D-CoE serves as a "school of practice," combining academic rigor with real-world applications. Key pillars include:

Experienced Faculty

IISc’s seasoned educators ensure that the curriculum is innovative and industry-aligned.

Hands-On Learning

Programs emphasize practical training, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and market demands.

A Nodal Agency for Innovation

Beyond education, the D-CoE plays a vital role in fostering innovation and collaboration within Karnataka’s ecosystem. It:

Supports MSMEs and Corporates

Through training in design thinking, prototyping, and Industry 4.0 technologies, it empowers businesses to thrive in a connected world.

Promotes Collaboration

Acts as a bridge between academia, industry, and startups, creating a dynamic environment for innovation.

Previous Broadcasts Overview

Broadcast 1: Introduction to the D-CoE

The Design Centre of Excellence (D-CoE) at IISc Bangalore was introduced as a collaboration with the Government of Karnataka. Positioned as a "School of Practice for Design," the D-CoE aims to transform Karnataka's innovation ecosystem by integrating academia, industry, and startups.

Broadcast 2: Addressing Startup Challenges

Highlighted the D-CoE's role in tackling the challenges faced by India's startup ecosystem, where 80% of startups fail within five years. The D-CoE addresses these challenges by equipping startups, MSMEs, and the workforce with advanced skills in Industry 4.0 technologies, prototyping, and business development.

D-CoE Shaping the Future of Design

With its roots in IISc's rich history and its focus on the future, the D-CoE is uniquely positioned to redefine design education and practice. By combining tradition with modernity, it serves as a beacon for aspiring designers, industry professionals, and innovators alike.

The D-CoE is more than an institution; it is a movement that continues IISc's legacy of excellence while charting new paths in the ever-evolving world of design.
